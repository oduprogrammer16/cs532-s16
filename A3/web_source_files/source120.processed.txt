#6 Identify Any Infections

   Identify Any Infections

   If you want to eliminate vaginal odor as quickly as you can, you need
   to figure out the source of the smell. The most common cause of odor is
   an infection. This can range from yeast infections, bacterial
   infections and STDs/STIs.
