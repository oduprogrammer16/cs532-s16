10 Genetic Traits You Definitely Weren’t Aware Of

   [15]Viral Cycle [16]Facts, [17]Health
   [INS: :INS]

   Two things are responsible for human development: environmental
   influence and genetics, the latter having the greater influence.
   Although obvious things are influenced by genetics, such as athletic
   ability and intelligence, certain genetic traits are not as obvious.

10. Laziness

   lazy

   Some people are abnormally lazy, preferring to laze on the couch and
   watch movies rather than working. They just cannot seem to shake this
   habit. It is not necessarily their fault, however, as genetics can play
   a role.

   Researchers who bred two groups of rats found a laziness gene by
   interbreeding active and less active rats respectively. Over a few
   generations, it was found that those with active predecessors could run
   for longer periods of time, while those whose ancestors were less
   active, ran for a lessor period. They found that there were many
   factors ascribed to genetics, such as mitochondria levels, physical
   characteristics, etc. The greatest reason for their activity levels,
   however, was found in a set of 36 genes.

   Laziness, therefore, may have an excuse.
