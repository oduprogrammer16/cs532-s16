Sunday Jazzy Champagne Brunch Cruise

   [40]Community Scene's picture
   Submitted by [41]Community Scene on February 8, 2016 - 7:45pm

If you like this page, please share it with others:

   Sunday, February 21, 2016 - 4:00pm

   Stunning views of Manhattan, a live jazz band, mimosas, a brunch buffet
   and more make for a signature Sunday in New York City aboard this
   special Hornblower Cruise. Aside from the beautiful vistas of the
   world's most famous skyline, this two-hour trip offers a wide selection
   of breakfast and lunch favorites, including omelets, charcuterie, a
   carving station, a dessert station and signature coffee and teas.
   There's a cash bar on board, but if you spring for a premier ticket,
   your mimosas are included.

   Full Price: $44.09 - $95.26

   Goldstar Price: $27.00 - $57.00
