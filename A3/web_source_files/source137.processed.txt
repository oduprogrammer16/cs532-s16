How To Make Mexican Shredded Beef!!

   17:12Mouth-watering Recipes

   [6][Mexican_shredded_beef_2.jpg]
   [EMBED]
   Today we’re gonna make together
   Mexican shredded beef
   The ingredients
   1.   One and a half boneless beef chuck roast
   2.   One medium onion (sliced)
   3.   One cup beef broth
   4.   1 (1 and quarter ounce) package taco seasoning mix
   5.   1 jar taco sauce about 16 ounces
   6.   1  diced green chilies about 7 ounces
   The procedures
   1.   Place beef roast and onion into the slow cooker. In a small bowl
   combine taco seasoning and beef broth. Pour over the beef and onion.
   Cover and cook until tender. Shred the beef using two forks.
   2.   Place the beef in a large bowl. Stir in taco sauce and green
   chilies. Use the shredded beef for whatever you like… quesadillas,
   tacos or burritos.
   3.   And enjoy your meal.
