<p style="text-align: center;">To start, I pulled everything out of my drawers and began to sort.  Here is a before of my sock drawer which was overflowing.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9379" src="http://i2.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/IMG_4476-e1454277100419.jpg?resize=2448%2C3264" alt="IMG_4476" srcset="http://i2.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/IMG_4476-e1454277100419.jpg?resize=225%2C300 225w, http://i2.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/IMG_4476-e1454277100419.jpg?resize=768%2C1024 768w, http://i2.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/IMG_4476-e1454277100419.jpg?w=2448 2448w" sizes="(max-width: 2448px) 100vw, 2448px" data-recalc-dims="1" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Next, after sorting and getting rid of old, worn or not needed items, I cleaned out the dust from the drawer and set up my newly purchased inserts.  These are a set of drawer organizers that I purchased from IKEA.  They open up and can be configured in a variety of ways.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><em><span style="font-size: 10pt;">This post contains affiliate links, see full disclosure <a href="http://mylifefromhome.com/disclosure-privacy/">here.</a></span></em></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9342" src="http://i2.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4477.jpg?resize=3264%2C2448" alt="IMG_4477" srcset="http://i2.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4477.jpg?resize=300%2C225 300w, http://i2.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4477.jpg?resize=1024%2C768 1024w, http://i2.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4477.jpg?w=3264 3264w" sizes="(max-width: 3264px) 100vw, 3264px" data-recalc-dims="1" /></p>

<p style="text-align: center;">Drawer Organizers: <a href="http://api.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?id=477356138&amp;pid=uid7456-29895384-6">IKEA</a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">The next step was to fold the items in the way that was recommended by the Kon Mari book.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Step 1: Lay flat on top of each other</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9343" src="http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4478.jpg?resize=3264%2C2448" alt="IMG_4478" srcset="http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4478.jpg?resize=300%2C225 300w, http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4478.jpg?resize=1024%2C768 1024w, http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4478.jpg?w=3264 3264w" sizes="(max-width: 3264px) 100vw, 3264px" data-recalc-dims="1" /> <img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9344" src="http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4479.jpg?resize=3264%2C2448" alt="IMG_4479" srcset="http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4479.jpg?resize=300%2C225 300w, http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4479.jpg?resize=1024%2C768 1024w, http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4479.jpg?w=3264 3264w" sizes="(max-width: 3264px) 100vw, 3264px" data-recalc-dims="1" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Step 2: Fold in half</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9345" src="http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4480.jpg?resize=3264%2C2448" alt="IMG_4480" srcset="http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4480.jpg?resize=300%2C225 300w, http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4480.jpg?resize=1024%2C768 1024w, http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4480.jpg?w=3264 3264w" sizes="(max-width: 3264px) 100vw, 3264px" data-recalc-dims="1" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Step 3: One more fold to make a small square</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9346" src="http://i1.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4481.jpg?resize=3264%2C2448" alt="IMG_4481" srcset="http://i1.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4481.jpg?resize=300%2C225 300w, http://i1.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4481.jpg?resize=1024%2C768 1024w, http://i1.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4481.jpg?w=3264 3264w" sizes="(max-width: 3264px) 100vw, 3264px" data-recalc-dims="1" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Step 4: Insert into drawer vertically like little cards</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9348" src="http://i1.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4483-e1454276495917.jpg?resize=2448%2C3264" alt="IMG_4483" srcset="http://i1.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4483-e1454276495917.jpg?resize=225%2C300 225w, http://i1.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4483-e1454276495917.jpg?resize=768%2C1024 768w, http://i1.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4483-e1454276495917.jpg?w=2448 2448w" sizes="(max-width: 2448px) 100vw, 2448px" data-recalc-dims="1" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">This seems time-consuming, but in reality, it did not take more than an extra couple of minutes total and the result was fantastic.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-9349 size-full" src="http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4484-e1454277760949.jpg?resize=3264%2C2448" alt="IMG_4484" srcset="http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4484-e1454277760949.jpg?resize=300%2C225 300w, http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4484-e1454277760949.jpg?resize=1024%2C768 1024w, http://i0.wp.com/mylifefromhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/IMG_4484-e1454277760949.jpg?w=3264 3264w" sizes="(max-width: 3264px) 100vw, 3264px" data-recalc-dims="1" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Not only did it create more room, but you can see everything you have in your drawer.  It is neat and organized.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">I tackled my sock and underwear/ bra drawers.  I&#8217;ll spare you from seeing my undies, but I feel like I&#8217;m shopping at VS every time I open my drawer now.  What a great feeling!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">This made a huge difference and I can&#8217;t wait to tackle additional drawers in the same way!</p>
<h2 style="text-align: left;"><b>To life feeling like home!</b></h2>
