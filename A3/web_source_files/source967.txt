  <div class="clearfix event-details-header"><h3 class="artist-name">Kokayi</h3>    <ul class="external-links">

						    <li class="external-links-twitter"><a href="http://www.twitter.com/kokayi" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Twitter</a></li>

										    <li class="external-links-facebook"><a href="https://www.facebook.com/KokayiMusic" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Facebook</a></li>

      </div>

<p class="bio description">Grammy nominated, artist/producer, Kokayi's latest record Pro Deo et Patria was voted: Best records of 2012 by Boldaslove.us,  #6 of best records of 2012 by the Washington City Paper and # 3 best indie record of 2012 by RoxFontaine.com.  The lead single Birdus Ghetti for Pro Deo et Patria was featured as an exclusive download on MTV Hive.com.  His songs can be heard in Kevin Hart's "Laugh at my Pain" and an original composition appears in the Bernie Mac documentary "I Ain't Scared of You".  Kokayi's record Robots & Dinosaurs received a 93/100 from the taste makers at Okayplayer.com, with the video "RoxTar" from that record winning "The Freshmen" contest for MTVU. <br />
<br />
As an educator, he has lectured and facilitated workshops with youth groups and at Colleges and Universities in the United States, Europe, China, Mongolia, Russia, Jordan, Saudi Arabia, Egypt, Israel, Palestine, Bahrain, Cuba, Brazil, Argentina and Canada.  Kokayi is guest faculty with the School of Improvisational Music at NYU and has worked with the US State Department providing cultural exchange and collaboration with the programs; The Rhythm Road: Artists Abroad and 1beat fellowship.</p></div>
