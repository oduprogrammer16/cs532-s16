		<header class="cp-articleHeader">
	    	<h1>Beyoncé set for SoL show</h1>
            
	    	<p>
                Published: <time id="content_1_BylineTime" datetime="2016-02-08">08 February, 2016</time><br />
				by Kate Smith
	    	</p>
            
		</header>
        <p>Beyoncé to perform at the home of SAFC this summer! </p>
        <p style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">Beyonc&eacute;, one of the biggest female artists of all time, is set to light up the North East in the summer following the announcement of her hotly-anticipated world tour.</p>
<p style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">Recognised as the most powerful woman in the music industry and Billboard&rsquo;s Artist of the Millennium, Beyonc&eacute; will take to the stage at Sunderland&rsquo;s Stadium of Light in what will be the prestigious opening night of the European leg of the Formation World Tour, on Tuesday 28 June, 2016.</p>
<p style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">The show will be the iconic singers&rsquo; first-ever stadium performance in the North East, with Sunderland selected as one of only five UK venues to host the global superstar and one of only two stadium venues in England, the other being Wembley.</p>
<p style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">The Crazy in Love star is heralded as one of the biggest and best entertainers in the world and one of the most successful female artists of all time. She has sold over 118 million records as a solo artist and a further 60 million with her former band, Destiny&rsquo;s Child.</p>
<p style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">The Formation World Tour is Beyonc&eacute;&rsquo;s first solo tour since her ambitious Mrs. Carter Show World Tour in 2013. The sold-out tour spanning two years and five continents thrilled nearly two million fans and was hailed by critics worldwide. During the summer of 2014, Beyonc&eacute; and Jay Z launched the six-week On The Run Tour, playing major stadiums in the US. They ended the tour in Paris, France in September 2014 with two additional dates that were filmed for an HBO Special that was nominated for an Emmy Award.</p>
<p style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">Speaking about the concert, Sunderland AFC&rsquo;s commercial director, Gary Hutchinson, said: &ldquo;This concert represents a huge win not only for the Stadium of Light but the North East region too. Given Beyonc&eacute;&rsquo;s iconic status in today&rsquo;s music scene, this is without doubt the biggest concert announcement we have ever made.</p>
<p style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">&ldquo;In a career spanning almost 20 years, she has consistently produced global hits and is one of the greatest artistes in recent history. To have her perform here in Sunderland, in her prime, will be a once-in-a-lifetime experience for North East music fans and we can&rsquo;t wait to welcome her to the city and the stadium.&rdquo;</p>
<p style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;">Tickets will be priced from just &pound;43.50, but with unprecedented demand expected, fans are advised to secure their seat what will be the hottest show of the summer as soon as possible.</p>
<p style="line-height: 150%;"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">HOW TO BUY</span></strong><br />
Tickets will go on sale at 9am on Tuesday 16 February and will be available from the Stadium of Light ticket office and approved ticket agents. </p>
<p style="line-height: 150%;"><strong>Stadium of Light sales information:</strong><br />
The Stadium of Light will offer <strong>General Admission </strong>(standing/Tier 1 unreserved seating) tickets only to personal callers, priced at &pound;73.50 and a limited number of Gold Circle tickets at &pound;108.50. All sales are subject to a &pound;4 booking fee per ticket (unless payment is made in cash). Tickets cannot be purchased from the Stadium of Light box office by telephone or online. Tickets are available from the stadium&rsquo;s box office to in-person purchasers only.</p>
<p style="line-height: 150%;"><strong>Ticket agents:</strong><br />
General admission tickets (standing/Tier 1 unreserved seating) &pound;73.50. Tier 2 reserved seats &pound;73.50 and &pound;108.50 and Tier 3 reserved seats &pound;43.50/ &pound;63.50/&pound;73.50. All tickets are subject to a booking fee. Golden Circle tickets are priced at &pound;108.50.</p>
<p style="line-height: 150%;">Online at <a href="http://www.livenation.com">www.livenation.com</a>,&nbsp; <a href="http://www.ticketmaster.co.uk">www.ticketmaster.co.uk</a> or 0844 8440444</p>
<p style="line-height: 150%;"><a href="http://www.beyonce.com">www.beyonce.com</a> </p>
<p style="line-height: 150%;"><strong>Wheelchair and special needs patrons:</strong><br />
Tickets for wheelchair and special needs patrons are available by telephone from SAFC, tel. 0191 551 5004 or <a href="mailto:ticket.office@safc.com">ticket.office@safc.com</a> </p>
<p style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;"><strong>Important information:</strong><br />
Young people aged 14 and under must be accompanied by an adult aged over 18 and no unaccompanied youngsters aged 14 or under will be permitted entry to the venue. </p>
<p style="line-height: 150%;">There is a limit of <strong>eight</strong> tickets per purchaser in place.</p>
<p style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">HOSPITALITY</span></strong><br />
Demand for this show is expected to be exceptionally high. Call <strong>0871 911 1555</strong> or email <a href="mailto:beyoncehosp@safc.com">beyoncehosp@safc.com</a> to find out more information or to make an expression of interest for our exciting new concert hospitality concept or view <a href="~/link.aspx?_id=6B455D0A7CA54939820437AB2D39E142&amp;_z=z">here.</a>&nbsp; Existing SAFC hospitality customers should contact the club immediately to reserve their package. </p>
<p style="line-height: 150%; text-align: justify;"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">HILTON GARDEN INN</span></strong><br />
A new 141 bedroom Hilton Garden Inn will open adjacent to the Stadium of Light in April 2016. For more information or to make a room reservation call 0191 551 5110 or email <a href="mailto:Catherine.graham@hilton.com">Catherine.graham@hilton.com</a>, prompt booking advised.</p>
