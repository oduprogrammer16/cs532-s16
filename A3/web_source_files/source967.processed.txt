Kokayi

     * [32]Twitter
     * [33]Facebook
     * [34]Website

   Grammy nominated, artist/producer, Kokayi's latest record Pro Deo et
   Patria was voted: Best records of 2012 by Boldaslove.us,  #6 of best
   records of 2012 by the Washington City Paper and # 3 best indie record
   of 2012 by RoxFontaine.com. The lead single Birdus Ghetti for Pro Deo
   et Patria was featured as an exclusive download on MTV Hive.com. His
   songs can be heard in Kevin Hart's "Laugh at my Pain" and an original
   composition appears in the Bernie Mac documentary "I Ain't Scared of
   You". Kokayi's record Robots & Dinosaurs received a 93/100 from the
   taste makers at Okayplayer.com, with the video "RoxTar" from that
   record winning "The Freshmen" contest for MTVU.
   As an educator, he has lectured and facilitated workshops with youth
   groups and at Colleges and Universities in the United States, Europe,
   China, Mongolia, Russia, Jordan, Saudi Arabia, Egypt, Israel,
   Palestine, Bahrain, Cuba, Brazil, Argentina and Canada. Kokayi is guest
   faculty with the School of Improvisational Music at NYU and has worked
   with the US State Department providing cultural exchange and
   collaboration with the programs; The Rhythm Road: Artists Abroad and
   1beat fellowship.

The Walking Sticks

     * [35]Twitter
     * [36]Facebook
     * [37]Website

   Powerful female lead vocals and sibling harmony define the Walking
   Stick's sound. Twin brothers Max and Spencer Ernst anchor the grooves
   with symbiotic synth and guitar lines, while Chelsea Lee's melodies
   weave through the mix, inspiring moments of "dream pop bliss" - Indie
   Shuffle
