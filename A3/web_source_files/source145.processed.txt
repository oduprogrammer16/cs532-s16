£2 MILLION a year lost on gambling machines in Liverpool’s Williamson Square

   [printer_icon.gif] Print this page

   Punters lost nearly £2m on gambling machines dubbed the ‘crack cocaine
   of gambling’ last year.

   Gamblers racked up the heavy losses at 52 fixed-odds betting terminals
   (FOBTS) across just 13 bookies in or near Williamson Square
   in [22]Liverpool city centre.

   Read the full story in the [23]Liverpool Echo.
