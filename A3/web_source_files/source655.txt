        <h1 class="single-post-title">Home Sweet Home</h1>
        <div class="author-intro">		<img src="http://coxlesscrew.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/coxless-crew-laura-penhaul.png" alt="Laura Penhaul" class="avatar avatar- wp-user-avatar wp-user-avatar- alignnone photo" />						By <a href="http://coxlesscrew.com/author/laura/" title="Posts by Laura Penhaul" rel="author">Laura Penhaul</a>		<div class="post-entry-date-single">On 8 Feb 2016 in <a href="http://coxlesscrew.com/category/uncategorized/" rel="category tag">Uncategorized</a></div>				</div>
		
                        
                
<div class="main-post-area">

		<input class="jpibfi" type="hidden" ><p>Since arriving into Heathrow and receiving such a warm welcome from my wonderful family and friends, the week has flown by with a whirlwind of amazing media and meeting some lovely people and celebs. But regardless of all the hype and the make overs, there&#8217;s nothing better than being with family. First stop for me was to head down to Overton to stay with my brother and his family for a couple of days and more importantly spend some quality time with my gorgeous niece! Isla is now 3 years old and I think I can safely say she is one of the funniest people I know. Such a character and it was so special to have her run up to me as I got off the train after being away for so long. She certainly gives a great hug!</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-3881" src="http://coxlesscrew.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/image3-e1454948039794-225x300.jpg" alt="image3" width="225" height="300" /></p>
<p>From the first day I stepped onto Doris in San Francisco, I started dreaming of the time I would get home post row. If there were difficult times out there, one of my coping strategies I drew up with Keith our Sport Psych, was a distraction technique where I would mentally imagine the drive home from leaving the A30 to arriving at the front door. So last night when I was in the car with mum and dad and we were doing exactly the route I had envisaged over the last 9 months, once again the emotions hit me. Pulling up outside the front door, I was home sweet home in Cornwall, finally!<br />
It was about 6pm when we got back and with the house in darkness I had no idea that unbeknown to me, my whole family were hiding inside to surprise me!</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-3882" src="http://coxlesscrew.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/image4-e1454948015929-300x225.jpg" alt="image4" width="300" height="225" /><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-3883" src="http://coxlesscrew.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/image5-e1454947407548-300x225.jpg" alt="image5" width="300" height="225" /></p>
<p>Even my wonderful Auntie Marie (famous now for her amazing pasties!) was there with a pasty for us all and mine even had my name on it!</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-3884" src="http://coxlesscrew.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/image6-e1454947373750-225x300.jpg" alt="image6" width="225" height="300" /></p>
<p>The champagne flowed and I got a chance to catch up with my cousins and aunts and uncles before settling down in the evening to a log fire and just mum, dad and I in the lounge. Absolute bliss!</p>
<p>Today, mum &amp; dad have been re-acquainted with the media when ITV Westcountry came round to our home to interview us. Note how my dad still has his slippers on! No wonder I used to be embarrassed at 14 years old when he would pick me up from an under 18&#8217;s nightclub and he&#8217;d be wearing these! Shocker! Now he&#8217;s on local tv wearing them, priceless!</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-3886" src="http://coxlesscrew.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/image8-e1454947279594-300x225.jpg" alt="image8" width="300" height="225" /></p>
<p>If you live in the South West, then it should be being aired tomorrow (Tuesday 9.2.16) in the ITV Westcountry news at 6pm. Tomorrow I&#8217;m also heading up to the BBC studios in Plymouth where I&#8217;ll be live on the couch for an interview after 6pm also, so keep an eye out on our social media twitter and facebook for more up to date timings.</p>

</div>
