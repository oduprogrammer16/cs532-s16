<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">I did mention that you needed the right mindset, tools and systems right? Well here’s part of that not no secret formula. These 8 core steps should be used as a guideline or road map to build any business part time or full time.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">The first 4 steps are the action steps.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18pt;color: #000000"><strong>Core step #1 Get All In</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">All in means ALL IN. Mentally, physically, spiritually, financially and any other ly word you can think of. <p><span style="font-size: 14pt">It’s the attitude and confidence, mindset and framework all other business owners lack who are not all in. Here’s the litmus test…you ether figure out how to get it done or you make excuses.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">Bottom line is that you want to be a product of the product. If you sell Chevy&#8217;s you&#8217;re not going to drive a Dodge are you?</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18pt;color: #000000"><strong>Core Step #2 Blog Daily</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">Everyone has a voice and everyone has a story. And whether you are a micro blogger on Twitter or a video blogger on Youtube&#8230;you have the ability to share your story. This may be hard to swallow if you&#8217;re new but trust me&#8230;everyone has a story!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">Additionally blogging helps with many other aspects of your online business Examples are</span></p>

<li><span style="font-size: 14pt">Becoming a better writer</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt">Making better videos</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt">Communicating better</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt">Networking, both online and offline</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt">Learning and molding your skill sets</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt">Personal development</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt">Increase creativity and memory</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">And the list goes on. Never underestimate the power of blogging! Plus it beats out static websites like a million to one as far as social AND organic traffic. <a href="http://www.empowernetwork.com/almostasecret.php?id=kenpickard" target="_blank">Need a blog</a>?</span></p>

</strong></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt">What’s recommended by successful people.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt">Leadership and success.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt">Mindset and personal development.</span></li>

<p><span style="font-size: 18pt;color: #000000"><strong>Core Step #8 Attend The Next Event</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">One of the most important core commitments you can make when starting an online business is to get the next live event.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">The audios and perfect for you to rewire the brain during downtime.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">The books are perfect for start and or ending your day with information you chose.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">The weekly conference calls are great for staying plugged into the updates and gleaning some golden nuggets from the leaders.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">But there is nothing like the next live event. Flat out nothing! It’s the main event that is put on by either industry professionals and trainers or by a company your promoting. The best of the best is always delivered at the next event.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">I could write volumes on what it means top get to these events, but I leave you with a quick story.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">I was at an event several years ago (by the way you get THE BEST stories from events) and a speaker shared this success principal.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">“<a href="http://www.thenetworkdad.com/be-here-a-year-from-now/" target="_blank">Be here 1 year from now</a>.”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">Someone asked him then what. He said “Make a new commitment to be here a year from now.” He then went on to share that in the last 4 years the attendance of the live events doubled almost every year, but those who came back year after year were cut in half.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">So after 4 years there was a couple of tables in the back of the meeting hall with about 40 people. Each of them had been coming to every live event for the past 4 years and the average income was well over 100K a year.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">In our online business space every single successful person can attribute a shift in their business due to a live event!</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18pt;color: #000000"><strong>Final thoughts</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">Because I only want to build you up for success I’m going to come clean and shoot straight now. You are most likely finding yourself in one of two camps.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">The first being “Holy cow that’s a lot of work…not sure if I can do this.” I’m here to say that you can AND you absolutely deserve it to make it work. I first thought the same as you but noticed a shift when I started to weed things out of my life that didn’t serve me, like my business can. When I did that I realized I had more time to commit to the things I needed to do.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">The second camp being “This is the road map I’ve been looking for to <a href="http://www.empowernetwork.com/almostasecret.php?id=kenpickard" target="_blank">build my online business </a>thanks for the marketing plan I got this, and am ready for it pay me like a full time business should.&#8221; I say welcome to the new you!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">If you’re in the first camp I would encourage you to look at your why…your goals and see if they are worth the fight. There’s nothing wrong with staying where you are if you only want to stay comfortable. Even if that means paycheck to paycheck.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">But when you’re ready for more…ready to build a life by design and not settle for the same ol default life…then you’re ready to step up and make that decision that your goals are worth the effort.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt">If you’re in the second camp and you know that you know you’re going to do whatever it takes, even if you don’t know how just yet…then welcome to your new future. The life you want wants you and there’s nothing to hold you back.</span></p>

For the price of lunch for 2 you can have access to the very information that has been the foundation of many 6 and 7 figure businesses. Each of these core commitments has a video tutorial with detailed information on how to get the most out of the commitment and why it’s important to your business.</span></p>
