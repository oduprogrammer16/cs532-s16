London Engagement photos – Hannah & Simon

   [42]February 8, 2016 [43]Engagements


   London Engagement photos

A beautiful spring day in London

South London Engagement photos with Hannah and Simon

   I was thrilled when Simon and Hannah asked me to photograph their
   special day. These two are such a fun and energetic couple! The
   engagement session especially was so relaxed and casual. They interact
   on the most intimate of levels; they are truly the best of friends. It
   was one of those ideal situations as a wedding photographer- there was
   so little prompting, the session flowed, and it was as if I was just
   out for a stroll with them and happened to bring a camera along. Made
   my job so simple!

   When deciding on the location for their engagement shoot, Hannah and
   Simon chose areas close to home, including Tooting Common and the
   street they live down with a quaint vintage rug store at the end of the
   road. The vibrant red door of the home they share together is a perfect
   unintentional symbol of their love and pride of ownership.
