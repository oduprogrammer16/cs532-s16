   [67]'Lip Sync Battle': Eva Longoria & Hayden Panettiere to duke it out

   upcoming episode of [70]Lip Sync Battle, where the Nashville star
   performs “Lady Marmalade” with the help of Aguilera herself.

   recorded a cover for the Moulin Rouge! soundtrack.
