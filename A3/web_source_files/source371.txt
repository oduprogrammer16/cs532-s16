<meta content='https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-T3EbOWu75SY/VsIR0L0f47I/AAAAAAAACuQ/4qKfyM7jjxw/s640/Photo%2BFeb%2B10%252C%2B6%2B02%2B30%2BPM.jpg' itemprop='image_url'/>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://www.brooke-elise.com/2016/02/collective-haul.html'>collective haul</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-6195786033061570206' itemprop='articleBody'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-T3EbOWu75SY/VsIR0L0f47I/AAAAAAAACuQ/4qKfyM7jjxw/s1600/Photo%2BFeb%2B10%252C%2B6%2B02%2B30%2BPM.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img alt="colourpop kathleen lights colourpop where the night is weenie porter telepathy trust me bossy lumiere 2 lumiere lippie pencil" border="0" height="480" src="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-T3EbOWu75SY/VsIR0L0f47I/AAAAAAAACuQ/4qKfyM7jjxw/s640/Photo%2BFeb%2B10%252C%2B6%2B02%2B30%2BPM.jpg" title="" width="640" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-7dEACv5oKgY/VsIR0JulBkI/AAAAAAAACuM/jJN4OsaXobI/s1600/Photo%2BFeb%2B14%252C%2B6%2B38%2B49%2BPM.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img alt="too faced natural matte palette nest paradise nars radiant creamy concealer lush the kiss lip scrub" border="0" height="480" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-7dEACv5oKgY/VsIR0JulBkI/AAAAAAAACuM/jJN4OsaXobI/s640/Photo%2BFeb%2B14%252C%2B6%2B38%2B49%2BPM.jpg" title="" width="640" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<br />
You know, I'm really bad with spending money. Like, really bad. I'm a sucker for new Colourpop releases, and I'm a sucker for a good perfume and concealer. I'm weak.<br />
<br />
A couple weeks ago, Colourpop came out with a limited edition lipstick, in a new formula called <a href="https://colourpop.com/product/trust-me/"><span style="color: #073763;">Trust Me</span></a>. I knew that if I didn't pick it up, I would forever regret it, unless they released it as a part of their permanent line. Let me tell you, the formula of this is AMAZING. I'm really mad that I didn't pick up another one, because it's a great formula. I also picked up <a href="https://colourpop.com/product/bossy-pencil/"><span style="color: #073763;">Bossy lip pencil</span></a> to match.<br />
<br />
In that same order, I picked up some of Colourpop's collaborations with KathleenLights. I have her <a href="https://colourpop.com/product/where-the-light-is/"><span style="color: #073763;">orignal quad</span></a>, and that charmed the pants off of me. However, when I saw her second quad, <a href="https://colourpop.com/product/where-the-night-is/"><span style="color: #073763;">Where the Night Is</span></a>, I became very smitten. It doesn't have the most conventional colours, buuuuut it's a beauty. <a href="https://colourpop.com/product/weenie/"><span style="color: #073763;">Weenie</span></a> and <a href="https://colourpop.com/product/porter/"><span style="color: #073763;">Porter</span></a> are easily my favourite shades in this quad, but <a href="https://colourpop.com/product/telepathy/"><span style="color: #073763;">Telepathy</span></a> is a show stealer. Kathleen also came out with <a href="https://colourpop.com/product/lumiere-2/"><span style="color: #073763;">Lumiere 2</span></a>, the liquid lipstick version of her ever popular Lumiere lipstick. I picked up the <a href="https://colourpop.com/product/lumiere-pencil/">matching pencil</a>, and called it a day.<br />
<br />
On Valentine's Day, I couldn't make it without stopping at Sephora and Lush. As a Valentine's Day present, Cody picked me up the <a href="http://www.sephora.com/natural-matte-eye-palette-P393185?skuId=1664895"><span style="color: #073763;">Too Faced Natural Matte palette</span></a>. I've been after this palette for a while, and it's just gorgeous. ALL THE WARM TONES.<br />
<br />
For myself, I picked up <a href="http://www.sephora.com/paradise-rollerball-P385315?skuId=1584994"><span style="color: #073763;">Nest Paradise</span></a> in a rollerball. I just had to take advantage of the triple fragrance points while I could. It smells amazing. Paradise is definitely more of a summer fragrance (top note is blood orange), but it'll perk me right up in the rainy weather that won't seem to cut me a break lately.<br />
<br />
My last two purchases were really impulses. I seem to be collecting concealers like it's no one's business lately, and I really wanted to try the <a href="http://www.sephora.com/radiant-creamy-concealer-P377873?skuId=1726058"><span style="color: #073763;">NARS Radiant Creamy Concealer</span></a>. I picked up a trial size version, to make sure I really loved it before I sprung for the full size. I don't know if the shade I picked up is an exact match, but I will make do. At Lush, I was fully intending on getting more than just <a href="http://www.lushusa.com/The-Kiss-Lip-Scrub/05662,en_US,pd.html"><span style="color: #073763;">The Kiss</span></a>, but I showed decent restraint. That, and they were out of the small sizes of the masks I like.<br />

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://www.brooke-elise.com/2016/02/our-anniversary.html'>our anniversary</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-7782486524775200817' itemprop='articleBody'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-Rdhm_pFBcoc/VsIPjau_-sI/AAAAAAAACt4/mWbEFolY2kQ/s1600/our%2Banniversary%2B2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="480" src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-Rdhm_pFBcoc/VsIPjau_-sI/AAAAAAAACt4/mWbEFolY2kQ/s640/our%2Banniversary%2B2.jpg" width="640" /></a><a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-GN0hfikKpoU/VsIPjV3V2SI/AAAAAAAACt8/HOsAO7-h3s0/s1600/our%2Banniversary%2B3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="480" src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-GN0hfikKpoU/VsIPjV3V2SI/AAAAAAAACt8/HOsAO7-h3s0/s640/our%2Banniversary%2B3.jpg" width="640" /></a></div>
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-VuJ57xCgBz8/VsIPdyNFQDI/AAAAAAAACt0/pID9nQRHmL8/s1600/our%2Banniversary.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="640" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-VuJ57xCgBz8/VsIPdyNFQDI/AAAAAAAACt0/pID9nQRHmL8/s640/our%2Banniversary.jpg" width="480" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
I realize that this is a little weird to post RIGHT after a post I made about a bad relationship I was in, but this was a really good day in my life. Yesterday was Cody's and my third anniversary. Yes, we're that couple that got together on Valentine's Day. I asked him out with a heart made out of sticky notes on his windshield.</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
For our first anniversary, we went all out and went up to Seattle. We went to the aquarium, the Great Wheel, Pike Place, all that jazz. It was a great day, but I was exhausted afterward. Last year, we just had a more chill anniversary, involving Sephora, a pizza and Ben &amp; Jerry's.</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
This year, we combined the two past years, slightly. We decided to have an early lunch, as the Valentine's dinner rush is a little insane here. We went to Red Robin (surprise, surprise...), and headed to Sephora and Lush afterward. Again, not really a surprise if you know us.</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
Deadpool is one of our favourite Marvel characters, so we were naturally very excited to go see the movie yesterday. We normally wait a few weeks until the hype has died down, but we just couldn't wait. It was fantastic, and I couldn't recommend it enough.</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
We then went to the store to pick up some flowers for my mom, and I bought us two mini pies to enjoy at home.</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
After that, it was pretty much just Disney movies on Netflix.&nbsp;</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
I couldn't have asked for a better anniversary. I didn't need fireworks, or anything they show in the movies. It was perfect, and I couldn't ask for a better boyfriend. I really appreciate Cody, and I love the guy to death.&nbsp;</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
<br /></div>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://www.brooke-elise.com/2016/02/to-my-teenage-self-in-abusive.html'>to my teenage self in an abusive relationship</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-7664813300960100659' itemprop='articleBody'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-yzrbFnh2N6I/VsIMsCALVEI/AAAAAAAACtg/bynlgxgrp4M/s1600/dear%2Bteenage%2Bself%2B.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><img border="0" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-yzrbFnh2N6I/VsIMsCALVEI/AAAAAAAACtg/bynlgxgrp4M/s1600/dear%2Bteenage%2Bself%2B.jpg" /></span></a></div>
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;"><br /></span>
<span style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;"><br /></span>
<span style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">Before we start with this post, I'm going to slap a huge content warning on this. This post details my time in an abusive relationship. Please read with caution. While my ex never hit me, he was very emotionally abusive, as well as manipulative. My story may be different than other abusive relationship stories, and everyone will experience a bad relationship differently. I wouldn't wish a bad relationship on anyone.</span></span><br />
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;"><br /></span>
<span style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">Dear teenage self,</span></span><br />
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">You will get caught up in the attention of one of your best friend's exes. she will call you a slut for dating her "sloppy seconds". You'll stop being her friend after that, because you realized she will soon start valuing the opinion of the boys she dates more than yours.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">This boy will be no good for you. It seems nice until the time he asks you how far you've gone, and he takes you farther than you wanted to go. It seems nice when he gives you your first kiss, and asked you to be his girlfriend while you were stargazing. It seems nice when he takes you to his house after you got sick at school after state testing. it seems nice until you have to start babysitting him, and he disappears for days at a time.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">It seems nice until you have to make a story up for the school security guard, and tell him you were sick of babysitting this boy. You didn't tell him you two broke up because he got what he wanted.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">This boy will hurt you. He will not hit you, but he will leave emotional scars that have still not healed to this day. He will try to control you, and try to turn it around on you whenever something is his fault. He will make you do things you don't want to do, and he will make you hate your mom while you're dating him. He will leave marks on you to tell the world that you are his, that you'll mistake as love.&nbsp;</span></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">It will hurt when he leaves you. but it will hurt even more when you realize that you never said yes, and no one will believe you because you two were dating. It will hurt even more when you realize one of your best friends knew he was hurting you, and you wouldn't let him do anything about it. It will hurt the most when you can't tell your mom about what happened.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">No one will believe you because everything looked fine on the outside. No one will believe you because they didn't pay attention to the warning signs. No one will believe you because YOU didn't pay attention to the warning signs. No one will believe you because it took you months to realize what happened.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">He will call you a liar. He will get upset, because you told him you wouldn't do what his ex did. Because he did the same thing to her that he did to you.</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">Saying "I will die without you" is not okay. Disappearing for days at a time is not okay. Forcing you to do something you don't want is not okay. Turning things around to make them seem like your fault is not okay. Manipulating you, and isolating you from your friends is not okay.&nbsp;</span><br />
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">You will lose friends in this relationship. You will lose friends that you thought would be there for you through this whole situation.</span></span><br />
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">He will break up with you for the first time over a text message. He will break up with you the second time over a text message. You two will patch things up the first time after a couple of hours, and your best friend thinks that you shouldn't. You should have listened to them. Because he will continue to hurt you.</span></span><br />
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">It's not your fault, he was the one that took advantages of your weaknesses. It's not your fault, he was the one that played the mind games. It's not your fault, because he was the one that didn't think he was doing anything wrong.</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">You showed and told him about your most vulnerable parts, and he took advantage of that. You told him things that you had never told anyone.You told him about the one time you self harmed, about the time your best friend committed suicide, and what really happened with your dad. He took advantage of that vulnerability and used it to his advantage.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">It will take a long time for you to feel safe again. It will take a long time for you to trust someone again. It will take a long time for you to heal. Take all the time you need, because it is a very long healing process. You will process this differently than other people will, and it will be hard to "just get over it". You need to take time to process this.</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">He will show up at the school he no longer attends to scare you. He will try to make friends with your friends, and he will try to snake his way back into your life. He will come back into your life months after the breakup, because he wants to "be friends". He will still try to have sex with you, and you'll get sick all over him one of the last times you see him. He will still try to convince you you two are together, but he won't be there for you in the same way that he used to be. When you get shot in the face with a firework, he won't be there to hold your hand and get you ice. When you're sitting with mutual friends, you won't laugh the same, and he won't look at you the same. He made you fall in love with him, and never felt the same way. He won't pretend anymore.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">You will see him at a comic book convention, and hide yourself in the quiet room for hours with one of your best friends. You will see him at the gas station. to this day, you will still have anxiety attacks when you see him.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">He will find the boyfriend you have now on Facebook, and send him vile things and tell him what a liar you are. He'll tell him he never loved you, among other crass things.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">It will take a long time for you to fully process everything. It will take a very long time to escape him.</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">It will take a long time to realise one of your closest friends in the world was in a horrible relationship at the same time. like you, she suppressed everything and didn't tell anyone.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">You will be okay. &nbsp;you may not feel okay&nbsp;tomorrow, but you will be okay down the road. You will find someone that loves you for who you are, and what you've been through.</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">It will take you a while to realize all relationships are different, healthy or not. Some people get out of their abusive relationships, and some don't. Some can't. Some can't make it out before it's too late.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Controlling relationships are not romantic.&nbsp;</span></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">It is still abuse if they try to manipulate you.&nbsp;</span><br />
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">It is still abuse if they try to fuck with your mind.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">It is still abuse if they spin everything to be your fault.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">It is still abuse if their family makes fun of you, in front of you.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">It is still abuse when they go out of their way to find you.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="color: #454545; font-size: 14px;">
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">You will feel upset for a long time. you will still have anxiety attacks when you see him five years later at the gas station.&nbsp;</span><br />
<span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="font-size: 14px;">
<div style="color: #454545;">
<span style="font-family: &quot;georgia&quot; , &quot;times new roman&quot; , serif;">You still get choked up talking about it.&nbsp;</span></div>
<span style="font-family: &quot;georgia&quot; , &quot;times new roman&quot; , serif;"><span style="color: #454545;"><br /></span>
