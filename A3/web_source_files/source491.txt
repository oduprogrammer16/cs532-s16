          <div class="article-text entry-content" data-permalink="https://www.pinknews.co.uk/2016/02/07/gay-ukip-member-quits-party-over-vile-nasty-homophobic-candidate/" data-id="134028">
      
          <p>A member of UKIP who was hopeful to become a candidate for the party in the London Assembly elections has quit.</p>
<p>Richard Hendron, who had hoped to stand for the party in the South West constitutency for the London Assembly elections, quit the party over a &#8220;vile, nasty, homophobic&#8221; candidate.                  
                                                                      <div data-str-native-key="17e53cb8" style="display: none;"></div>
                    
                     </p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-111188" src="https://6682-presscdn-26-26-pagely.netdna-ssl.com/images/2015/08/Richard-Hendron2.jpg" alt="Richard Hendron2" width="1179" height="670" srcset="https://6682-presscdn-26-26-pagely.netdna-ssl.com/images/2015/08/Richard-Hendron2-300x170.jpg 300w, https://6682-presscdn-26-26-pagely.netdna-ssl.com/images/2015/08/Richard-Hendron2.jpg 1179w" sizes="(max-width: 1179px) 100vw, 1179px" /></p>
<p>Yesterday PinkNews reported that, following a hustings on Friday, UKIP had selected Alan Craig as its candidate for the seat.
                                        
                    
                </p>
<p>Mr Craig, who was selected at a hustings on Friday evening, previously <a href="http://www.pinknews.co.uk/2011/11/08/religious-party-leader-rise-up-against-cultural-domination-of-the-gaystapo/">compared gay equality advocates to the invading forces of Nazi Germany.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.pinknews.co.uk/2012/09/25/uk-politician-criticises-stonewall-for-bigot-nomination-but-wants-to-attend-ceremony/">He was nominated</a> for Stonewall’s ‘Bigot of the Year’ award in 2012.</p>
<p>His competition, Richard Hendron, has now resigned from the party over Mr Craig&#8217;s candidacy.
                                        
                    
                                         </p>
<p>In a letter addressed to the UKIP party chair, he wrote: &#8220;It has become clear to me that UKIP is anything but a libertarian party. It is a party dominated and controlled by white middle-aged heterosexual men who do not believe in libertarian values, equality or valuing difference. It’s a party that’s becoming even more small minded, a party that goes out of its way to maintain the status quo, a party that at best tolerates Gays rather than accepts them, a party that attacks difference and undermines equality.&#8221;</p>
<p>Going on, Mr Hendron said he was &#8220;lost for words&#8221; when he realised UKIP had put up Alan Craig against him for the candidacy.</p>
<p>Noting Mr Craig&#8217;s history on LGBT issue, Mr Hendron writes: &#8220;There is no other way to describe Alan Craig other than a vile, nasty homophobic individual who is driven by hatred of the LGBT community. A quick Google search shows just how nasty he is.&#8221;                                   </p>
<p>He goes on to say that it is &#8220;astounding&#8221; that UKIP would put forward Mr Craig against a gay man and LGBT activist.</p>
<p>The letter concludes: &#8220;I have tried my hardest to fight for the party and LGBT issues but I have been met with a barrage of resistance at all levels. UKIP is not really interested in equality, the LGBT group do nothing other than tick a box for the party, they do not push for change in the party or support LGBT activists who are trying to make a change, they even refused my application to join the LGBT group stating that being gay and in UKIP does not automatically qualify you for membership of LGBT in UKIP.</p>
<p>&#8220;The LGBT group should be lobbying hard for change and stopping the likes of Alan Craig becoming a candidate. I have to agree with Tom Booker when he resigned as chairman of LGBT in UKIP, saying &#8216;UKIP is a cult that is too willing to entertain bigots&#8217;, and that LGBT in UKIP is nothing more than public relations stunt to shield the party against allegations of homophobia.&#8221;</p>
<p>The Former Christian Peoples Alliance leader, Craig defected to UKIP last year and was inserted as a candidate for Brent North over an existing local candidate.                   
</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133957" src="http://pinknews.co.uk/images/2016/02/Alan-Craig.jpg" alt="Alan Craig" width="670" height="715" /></p>
<p>However, just days after his selection last April, <a href="http://www.pinknews.co.uk/2015/04/12/exclusive-ukip-candidate-alan-craig-to-speak-at-gay-cure-event/">PinkNews revealed that Mr Craig was set to speak at a ‘gay cure’ conference</a>.</p>
<p>Though conference organisers dropped Mr Craig after the PinkNews coverage, UKIP refused to discipline him, claiming that “as a party that believes in freedom of speech he has the right to speak”.</p>
<p>It was rumoured that he would stand for UKIP as the mayoral candidate, <a href="http://www.pinknews.co.uk/2015/09/26/ukip-picks-gay-candidate-peter-whittle-to-run-for-mayor-of-london/">however the party selected  out gay candidate Peter Whittle to run instead.</a></p>
<p>Mr Hendron, who <a href="http://www.pinknews.co.uk/2015/06/27/ukip-lgbt-group-marches-at-london-pride-despite-being-banned/">organised the party’s controversial contingent</a> at the Pride in London parade in 2015, <a href="http://www.pinknews.co.uk/2015/08/13/gay-ukip-candidate-richard-hendron-announces-run-for-mayor-of-london/">confirmed to PinkNews</a> last year that he planned to seek the party’s nomination to run for Mayor.</p>
<p>The barrister and former police officer, stood for Parliament in Brentford and Isleworth last May – and is not afraid to criticise his own party.</p>
<p>In 2015, he <a href="http://www.pinknews.co.uk/2015/08/01/ukip-overflowing-with-homophobes-claims-member/">warned that the party is perceived as</a> “overflowing with homophobes, racist and bigots” – adding that Nigel Farage’s remarks about HIV during the election were “indefensible” and “did no one any favours”.</p>
<p>The party <a href="http://www.pinknews.co.uk/2015/08/28/ukip-trying-to-block-gay-candidate-from-running-for-mayor-of-london/">was later accused</a> of attempting to block Mr Hendron from becoming the mayoral candidate because of the comments.                                      </p>
</p>
