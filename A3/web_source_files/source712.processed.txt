Jay Z Sent Beyonce 10,000 Roses Before Super Bowl Performance — So Sweet

   Mon, February 8, 2016 10:30am EST by [33]Kathleen Harper [34]4 Comments
   13,426 Article Views
     *
     *
     *
     *

   [35]View Gallery
   81 Photos

Talk about crazy in love! Before Beyonce’s big night on stage at Super Bowl
50, her hubby, Jay Z, surprised the superstar with thousands of roses! We’ve
never heard of a more adorable way to say ‘Break a leg!’ Does Bey have the
most romantic husband or what?!

   When [36]Beyonce, 34, is your wife, you know you have to go big or go
   home when it comes to showing her you care, and [37]Jay Z, 46,
   DEFINITElY knows how to shower his woman with love. To celebrate
   Beyonce’s halftime performance, her husband doused her in flowers. But
   of course he didn’t go the traditional route because nothing but the
   best for Queen Bey, right? Forget a dozen roses — this lucky lady got
   10,000!

   Before Beyonce hit the stage and gave the [38]Super Bowl 50 audience
   [39]a performance of a lifetime, the superstar was given a GIFT of a
   lifetime by her husband of almost eight years. Jay Z sent his love
   10,000 roses to celebrate her big night, according to [40]Us Weekly.
   Talk about a grand romantic gesture!

   Roses may just be Beyonce’s good luck charm too because the [41]“Drunk
   in Love” singer ROCKED the Super Bowl stage! She performed alongside
   [42]Bruno Mars and headlining act Coldplay at Levi’s Stadium in Santa
   Clara, California on Feb. 7. Blowing fans away with her fierce dance
   moves and killer vocals, Bey belted out her newest track,
   [43]“Formation.” No doubt the queen was on FIRE! Maybe Jay Z’s roses
   helped give her an extra spark!

   [44]Join Amazon Prime For Free – Thousands of Movies & Shows Anytime

   Beyonce has already been having one heck of a year. On Feb. 6, just one
   day before her second Super Bowl performance, the queen [45]released a
   sick music video for “Formation” with no notice! And as if that wasn’t
   enough, after slaying the halftime show, Beyonce had one more surprise
   in store for all of us. She announced that she’s going on tour! [46]The
   Formation tour will kick off on April 27, and so far 40 dates have been
   set but more are coming! Tickets go on pre-sale Feb. 9 while regular
   sales open on Feb. 16. [47]Find out all the dates here!

   There’s no doubt that Beyonce is pretty much winning at life right now
   — not to mention dominating the entertainment industry. And we just
   love how her sweet hubby is standing by her side and giving her endless
   amounts of love and support!
