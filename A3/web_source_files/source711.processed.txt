     The Downbeats

The Downbeats

   [10]The Downbeats Photo credit: Daniel Funaki

   The Downbeats are the youngest Wild Records sensation, they are a
   1950's inspired Rock N Roll band. The set consist of mainly original
   material. The band released their first recorded material on the Wild
   Party Vol. 2 10' inch record the end of 2014 and their first full
   length record Feb 2015. The Downbeats undertake their first European
   tour August 2015.

Additional Info

     * Band members:
       Sebestian Ballesteros - vocals, guitar
       Ricky Aguilera - lead guitar
       Robert Greenig - drums
       Sam Arreola - upper bass
     * Band location: Los Angeles, CA
