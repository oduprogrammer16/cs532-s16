<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
Guru - Hardwork Overlooked By VGMA
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-939511252494311366' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-cUWM7TzyX5I/VrjN61VE16I/AAAAAAAAJFs/TPBv4B9JYGw/s1600/guru4.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;"><img border="0" height="400" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-cUWM7TzyX5I/VrjN61VE16I/AAAAAAAAJFs/TPBv4B9JYGw/s400/guru4.jpg" width="335" /></a></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Over the years, even though the award winning hit maker Guru been consistent with dropping hits after hits, being given all his deserving awards for those hit songs at the Vodafone Ghana Music Awards never happened even though in certain instances it was obvious he deserve to win certain categories.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
This situation has gotten so serious to a point that people who are not even Guru fans have seen that he has not been treated fairly over the years.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
And from his point of view he deserves the artist of the year for 2016</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
with a picture of Guru, Ransford Kumi &nbsp;on his face book page wrote;</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
&#8220;Music Awards in Ghana Have not been fair to Guru especially VGMA.Even though am not a fan of Guru but Personally I think the Lapaz Toyota hit maker for the pass 3 years has put in much effort in his works and the music industry as a whole..Well,from my point of view,i think Guru deserves the 2016 artist of the year Award if Shatta Wale is not nominated&#8221;</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-xEnAdHkrHtI/VrjN898uTWI/AAAAAAAAJFw/p_PiysTbfDo/s1600/kumi-fansss.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="638" src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-xEnAdHkrHtI/VrjN898uTWI/AAAAAAAAJFw/p_PiysTbfDo/s640/kumi-fansss.jpg" width="640" /></a></div>
<br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
VGMA 2015 will be held soon and like Kumi wrote we hope he will be treated fairly by awarding all his deserving awards.</div>
<div style='clear: both;'></div>
