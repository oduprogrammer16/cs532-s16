What is Top 5 Followers?

   Top 5 Followers is a free app used to find your top 5 followers on

   This app encourages your followers to enter into funny competition to

How to get more followers on Twitter

   Here are some tips to get more followers on twitter. The most basic
   idea to get more followers on twitter is to list out all the topics you
   find interesting, then search on twitter to find people with similar
   interests, follow those people, read their tweets regularly, at least
   once a day. Then create interesting and relevant replies to their
   tweets, so they will reply back and at some point they will follow you
   back.

   The simple idea above will get you a lot of real followers in a short
   time, we are sure it will not take more than one month to get lots more
   followers on twitter by using the above said method. It’s a really good
   way to get more real followers.

   Another method is to find the community of your favorite star, or
