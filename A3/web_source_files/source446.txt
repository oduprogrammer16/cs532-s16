		<div itemprop="articleBody">
		<p>Moving across country is a rite of passage (and a test of will) for millions of Americans each year.</p>
<p>But where are they all going?</p>
<p>Two of America’s leading truck rental companies, Penske and U-Haul, recently released separate analyses of where people are moving based on their own customer data.</p>
<p>While their methodology differs, both lists offer unique insights on the places where long-distance movers are flocking.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.sparefoot.com/self-storage/blog/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2016/02/uhaul.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13974" src="https://www.sparefoot.com/self-storage/blog/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2016/02/uhaul.jpg" alt="uhaul" width="580" height="387" /></a></p>
<h3>U-Haul’s Top 10 U.S. Growth Cities</h3>
<p>U-Haul determined its list using the net gain of incoming one-way rentals versus outgoing rentals for the calendar year 2015. There were a total of 1.7 million one-way U-Haul rentals last year.</p>
<p>The <a href="http://myuhaulstory.com/2016/02/05/us-growth-city-no-1-concord-calif-tops-u-haul-list-for-2015/">top city on U-Haul’s list</a>? Concord, CA—a city of more than 122,000 in the San Francisco Bay Area.</p>
<p>“In conjunction with a great location, Concord remains one of the few affordable cities in the Bay Area and is surrounded by cities that offer sustained job growth,” said James Bostwick, U-Haul Company of East Bay president.</p>
<p>Coming in second on U-Haul’s list was Roseville-Sacramento, CA , followed by Austin, TX. You can see the <a href="https://www.uhaul.com/Articles/About/4753/Concord-CA-is-No-1-US-Growth-City-of-2015-N-Carolina-Tops-All-States">rest here</a>.</p>
<p>U-Haul also analyzed net incoming truck rentals on a state level, naming <a href="http://myuhaulstory.com/2016/02/05/north-carolina-nabs-title-of-u-haul-no-1-growth-state/">North Carolina</a> the number one growth state.</p>
<h3>Penske Truck Rentals Top Moving Destinations</h3>
<p>Penske Truck Rentals considers one-way truck rental totals only for their list.</p>
<p>“Our own data, compiled from do-it-yourself moves across the country, mirror the country’s migration and areas of growth,” said Don Mikes, Penske’s senior vice president of rental.</p>
<p>Penske customers made one-way moves to Atlanta more than any other city, for the second year in a row. Phoenix came in second on the list, followed by Tampa/Sarasota.</p>
<p>Check out the rest of the destinations below:</p>
<p><a href="http://blog.gopenske.com/news/penske-truck-rental-announces-2015-top-moving-destinations/"><img class="alignnone wp-image-13973 size-full" src="https://www.sparefoot.com/self-storage/blog/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2016/02/penskeinfo.jpg" alt="penskeinfo" width="580" height="4405" /></a></p>
