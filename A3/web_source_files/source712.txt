Forget a dozen roses &#8212; this lucky lady got 10,000!</p>

<p>Roses may just be Beyonce&#8217;s good luck charm too because the <a href="http://hollywoodlife.com/2013/12/13/beyonce-new-album-video/"  >“Drunk in Love”</a> singer ROCKED the Super Bowl stage! Blowing fans away with her fierce dance moves and killer vocals, Bey belted out her newest track, <a href="http://hollywoodlife.com/2016/02/06/beyonce-formation-music-video-song-blue-ivy-super-bowl-watch/"  >&#8220;Formation.&#8221;</a> No doubt the queen was on FIRE!
