<h3>South London Engagement photos with Hannah and Simon</h3>
<p>I was thrilled when Simon and Hannah asked me to photograph their special day. These two are such a fun and energetic couple! The engagement session especially was so relaxed and casual. It was one of those ideal situations as a wedding photographer- there was so little prompting, the session flowed, and it was as if I was just out for a stroll with them and happened to bring a camera along. Made my job so simple!</p>

The vibrant red door of the home they share together is a perfect unintentional symbol of their love and pride of ownership.</p>
