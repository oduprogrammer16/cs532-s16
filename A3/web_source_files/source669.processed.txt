German Meatballs

     * Posted on [9]February 8, 2016
     * by [10]Paula King
       This is one of our favorite main dishes. Since we raise our own
       pork and beef, the meat we use is always freshly ground. For
       variety, these meatballs can be cooked with a sweet cream gravy or
       steamed with tomatoes. But we prefer them with homemade sauerkraut.
       —Iona Redemer, Calumet, Oklahoma
