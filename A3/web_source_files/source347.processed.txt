NABTEB May/June 2016 Form, Online Registration in Progress

   Filed under  [41]Education
   [42]0
   [INS: :INS] [INS: :INS]

   [43]NABTEB May June 2016 Form - logo NABTEB May June 2016 Form - logo

   I wish to inform the general public that the [44]NABTEB May/June 2016
   Form and Online [45]Registration in on. Now every applicant need to go
   through the [46]NABTEB May/June 2016 Online [47]Registration making the
   process fast and easy.

How to Get NABTEB May/June 2016 Form

   The form is available in any [48]NABTEB office centers and the NABTEB
   [49]registration form/card cost N15,500 Only.  For those who can’t
   locate the Office or selling point for the online NABTEB scratch card
   can still get it here online on www.OnlineDailys.com.

   To get the Card sent to you make payment to the following account
   below:

   Bank Name: First Bank PLC
   Acct No: 3066151282
   Acct Name: NWABUZOR CHUKWUEMEKA
   [INS: :INS] [INS: :INS]

   Text your full name, state, phone number and Teller number to
   08032934651. For more information feel free to add us on Whatsapp chat
   Also Read this   NABTEB Headquarters, State Offices & Zonal Offices
   Address List

Steps to Register NABTEB Exam in Nigeria

    1. Visit c[50]http://mayjune.nabtebnigeria.org/ to Download Entry
       Guide
    2. Download Examination Structure (EA25)
    3. Download TA60
    4. Download TA61
    5. [51]Click here to start your NABTEB online registration for
       May/June 2016 Exam
    6. Now you will required to:  Enter your User Name
       Enter your Password
       Then Click Login


NABTEB Download instructions

   1. Make sure your passport is 160px by 200px and size is less than
   30kb.
   2. Click on the ‘Apply’ link.
   3. Fill in the PIN, and serial  number gotten from the purchased
   scratch card.
   4. Fill the form displayed and submit.
   5. Write down your Candidate Number in a safe place.

Things not to do on NABTEB Portal

   1. Do not use a card that has already been used for another candidate
   2. Do not enter an invalid e-mail address for [52]result to be sent as
   this will penalize you by deducting your card use
