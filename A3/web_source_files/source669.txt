	<div class="entry-content">
		<p> <br />
<br />This is one of our favorite main dishes. Since we raise our own pork and beef, the meat we use is always freshly ground. For variety, these meatballs can be cooked with a sweet cream gravy or steamed with tomatoes. But we prefer them with homemade sauerkraut. &mdash;Iona Redemer, Calumet, Oklahoma<img src="http://feeds.feedburner.com/~r/taste-of-home/recipe-of-the-day/~4/l7iQD3uA5e4" height="1" width="1" alt="" /><br />
