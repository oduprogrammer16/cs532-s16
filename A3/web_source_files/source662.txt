						<p>One of the best parts of any girls’ night is getting ready for it. It’s those hours that take you from woke-up-like-this to ready-for-the-dance-floor that set the mood for the kind of romance, glamour, mischief (or all of the above) you can count on once you walk out the door. But this beauty metamorphosis is arguably the most fun for supermodels and makeup experts <a href="http://www.vogue.com/tag/celebrity/kendall-jenner">Kendall Jenner</a> and <a href="http://www.vogue.com/tag/model/joan-smalls">Joan Smalls</a>, who, as the <a href="https://ad.doubleclick.net/ddm/clk/301147952;128090234;w" target="_blank">faces of Estée Lauder</a>, have every shade of lipstick, blush, nail polish, and mascara at their disposal. <a href="https://ad.doubleclick.net/ddm/clk/301147952;128090234;w" target="_blank">Complexion</a> looking a little dull? No problem. In search of a <a href="https://ad.doubleclick.net/ddm/clk/301147952;128090234;w" target="_blank">smoky eye</a> with a twist? Easy fix. Your best friend is feeling a red lip? <a href="https://ad.doubleclick.net/ddm/clk/301147952;128090234;w" target="_blank">There’s a matte for that</a>. We traced the duo from a fresh-faced evening prep in their hotel room to a fully polished, full-volume lip-synch in the backseat of their car. Watch what happens.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Kendall Jenner and Joan Smalls Hit the Hotel Suite</strong></p>
<p><iframe src="//player.cnevids.com/embed/56b122b394c05f260000001a/560c4b6461646d5ab0000004" width="560" height="390" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></iframe></p>
<p>For Kendall and Joan, inspiration starts with a tiara. They like to coordinate warm blush, nude lipstick, and jet black mascara, but when it comes to a smoky eye, it’s choose your own adventure.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Kendall Jenner and Joan Smalls Master the Mirror Check</strong></p>
<p><iframe src="//player.cnevids.com/embed/56b1242aff2afb645300004a/560c4b6461646d5ab0000004" width="560" height="390" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></iframe></p>
<p>Dress picked out, makeup did, manicure chip-free. One last mirror check and they know they look good—or rather, <em>major</em>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Kendall Jenner and Joan Smalls Throw a Backseat Dance Party</strong></p>
<p><iframe src="//player.cnevids.com/embed/56b1252994c05f2600000025/560c4b6461646d5ab0000004" width="560" height="390" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></iframe></p>
<p>When the music and the lipstick are loud enough, a traffic jam becomes the best party in town.</p>
