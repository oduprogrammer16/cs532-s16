        <p>James Martin Pedersen died Thursday. He played the role of "Rocky Rococo" for the chain of Rocky Rococo pizza restaurants for decades and lived in the Madison area.</p><p>Wayne Mosely, owner of the Madison Rocky Rococo franchise told News 3 that, as Rocky, Pedersen could relate to many groups of people.</p><p>"As Rocky, we valued his personal appearances because he could relate. He was adaptable."</p><p>"He became literally the face of Rocky. Everything was built around him," Mosely said. "He was a wonderful, talented guy."</p><p>Mosely said Pedersen did many other things in his career. He said Pedersen was with Second City in Chicago in the 1970s.</p></div>
