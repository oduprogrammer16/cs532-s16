   ***The entire Staten Island Staff LOVES this dog. He is a short and
   stocky meathead that thinks he is a lap dog. He came in with quite a
   few dogs and now is off of ID hold. He is a perfect gentleman with
   other dogs and has not shown any food aggression. PLEASE CONSIDER….***

   Came in with CODY – ID#A919531, FRANKIE A919521, FRANN – ID#A919523

   =======================================================================
   ===========

   FEBRUARY 2016

   PROFILE :

   Basic Information:

   Enzo is a 5 year old neutered Pitbull mix that has no health issues
   that the owner is aware of.

   Socialization:

   Enzo comes from a home with children and the previous owner stated he
   loves being around them. He plays with them in a gentle, playful
   manner. He has been around other cats and dogs before and was said to
   play with them and respect them, with no signs of aggression. He has
   never bitten another person or animal before.

   Behavior Issues:

   During storms or fireworks, Enzo is not bothered. The owner has never
   tried to bathe or clip his nails so his behavior is unknown. Enzo is
   not bothered when his food bowl is touched and will drop a toy is asked
   to do so. When someone unfamiliar approaches the yard or family member,
   Enzo is friendly and not bothered. He is not bothered having his coat
   brushed or being held or restrained.

   For a New Family to Know:

   Enzo is described as being confident, friendly, affectionate, playful
   and excitable with a high activity level. Enzo knows to sit and stay
   and when given belly rubs, he will roll over and accept them. When in
   the home, Enzo is the type to follow you around seeking attention and
   usually found in the same room as you are in. Enzo was kept indoors
   only and was fed 3 times a day dry food. The vet said that he was
   somewhat overweight so the previous owner cut down his food recently to
   only 2 meals per day. Enzo is house trained and has no accidents in the
   house. When left alone in the home, Enzo is well behaved. He has not
   been crate trained. He was taken for brisk walks on the leash and
   enjoys them. He usually went to the bathroom on the grass.
