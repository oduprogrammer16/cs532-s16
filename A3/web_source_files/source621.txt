				<h2 class="article-excerpt" itemprop="alternativeHeadline">The performance kicks off a busy February for the singer</h2>

					<span class="read-video-article collapsed">+ READ ARTICLE</span>
					<div class="video-content ">

<p>Lady Gaga kicked off Super Bowl 50 with a performance of the national anthem dressed in a custom sparkling red Gucci suit and matching eyeshadow.</p>
<p>Gaga called the gig a <a href="https://twitter.com/nflnetwork/status/695828049795874817?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">&#8220;dream come true&#8221;</a> in an interview with the NFL&#8217;s Nate Burleson that was published earlier this month. &#8220;I&#8217;ve always wanted to sing the national anthem at a major sporting event since I was a little girl,&#8221; she said. &#8220;And for it to be a Super Bowl&hellip;it is the ultimate.&#8221;</p>
<p>She joins a long list of pop stars&mdash;including Whitney Houston, Mariah Carey and Christina Aguilera&mdash;who have had the honors of singing before kickoff. Last year, <a href="http://time.com/3690543/idina-menzel-national-anthem-super-bowl/">Idina Menzel performed the song</a>.</p>
<p dir="ltr"><div class="galleryContainer galleryContainer--embedded" data-gallery="4188893" data-size="default"></div></p>
<p>The performance kicks off a big month for Gaga: she&#8217;ll <a href="http://time.com/4204149/lady-gaga-david-bowie-tribute-grammys/">perform a David Bowie tribute</a> at the Grammys on Feb. 15 and <a href="http://www.rap-up.com/2016/01/28/the-weeknd-lady-gaga-sam-smith-to-perform-at-oscars/">sing at the Oscars on Feb. 28 as well</a>.</p>
