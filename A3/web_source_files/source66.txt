<p>Coupled with how much the Obamas spent on other posh luxuries &#8212; $39,575.95 at Paradise Luxury Rentals; $34,827.57 at The Kahala; $209,218.25 at Cabana Girl LLC; $4,073,464.30 in flight expenses; etc. &#8212; they in total spent $5,316,522.10 for just two vacations.</p>
