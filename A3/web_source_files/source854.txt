                <p class="text-brief-description">
                    in Saint John's Wood, London.
                </p>
            <p>The big hard sun&nbsp;<br>blocked&nbsp;<br>by big hard buildings.&nbsp;<br>in St John&rsquo;s Wood.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Sunday afternoon, I walk.&nbsp;<br>Wondering what if this were a field<br>There would be so much sun&nbsp;<br>Shining soft all over&nbsp;<br>But instead.<br>I get glimpses of it through the cracks.&nbsp;</p>
<p>It&rsquo;s a gift&nbsp;<br>To notice it.&nbsp;<br>It softens our existence&nbsp;<br>in this hard world.</p>
